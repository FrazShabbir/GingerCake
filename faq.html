Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any concerns please read this collection of frequently asked questions before contacting
us. If you are still unclear about something feel free to contact.

How long do your cakes last?

Jasmine’s Jamaican Rum cakes are home-baked and freshly made and are best consumed within 3 -4 days of receipt. Store in a cool dry place. Serve at room temperature or slightly warm.

Can I purchase a whole cake?

We provide our 8 inch round whole cakes to our customers in the hospitality industry only. Please contact us for more information and if you would like to schedule a consultation where we can discuss your requirements further, we would be very happy to do so. Let’s be partners in helping our customers enjoy their special moment! 12 boxes of our Luxury sliced cakes are equivalent to one whole cake.

Does Jasmine Jamaican Rum cake contain allergens?

Our cakes contain the following allergens: Dairy, Gluten and Sulphite. Please note that Jasmine’s Jamaican Rum Cake is produced on premises that uses nuts.

How do I pay?

Payment can be made by credit or debit card. We accept payment with Mastercard or Visa. Payments are debited in Pounds (£) Sterling.

When can I expect my order?

Due to our freshly baked process, you can expect to receive your order 4 days after placing it. Please place all orders by 5pm local time. Any orders placed after 5pm will be processed the following day.
All deliveries will be posted out first class and will require a signature upon receipt.
Please note that we will not assume responsibility for any incorrect addresses provided to us.

Can I cancel my order?

As this is a perishable product we are unable to cancel or refund once an order has been placed.

Does Olga & Asquith share my information with others?

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so. We may use the information to improve our products and services.

What is Rum ?

Rum is a distilled alcoholic drink made by fermenting then distilling sugarcane molasses or sugarcane juice. 

How do I place multiple orders for business accounts?

At the moment we do not cater for corporate events.
